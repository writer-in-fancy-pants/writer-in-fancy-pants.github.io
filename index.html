Hi, I'm Mehul — a wandering researcher and software engineer, currently based in [India]. I am exploring a lot, specially at the multidisciplinary fringes, and going through the long list of tools and POCs that I had always meant to create for myself. I will also be sharing most of them freely here.

I care deeply about [open source, democratic access, human rights, climate change, preserving knowledge, and improving human condition], with focus on personally experiencing the reality as it is, as well as the spiritual experiences that arise through dedicated practice, like [excessive reading, dance, music, yoga, scuba diving, and meditation]. To that end, I am also a DJ, a producer, a licensed Yoga instructor, and often dance Salsa, Bachata, Zouk at socials and festivals.

Here, on this website, I plan to keep a record of my organized thoughts and my projects, on various topics. All the projects share a few key aspects - my personal interest, free tier AI tools, serverless, proof-of-concept, and privacy first. Feel free to reach out — I enjoy interesting conversations.
